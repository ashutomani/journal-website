Aims & Scope

Welcome to the Journal of Applied and Integrative Biology (JAIB). JIB is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to fostering innovative research across the spectrum of life sciences. As a multidisciplinary platform, JIB encourages the integration of knowledge from diverse fields to enhance understanding of biological systems in their complexity, from molecular to ecological scales.

JAIB publishes articles only in the English language.

At JAIB, we aim to provide a platform for scientists, researchers, and scholars to share high-quality research that pushes the boundaries of biology. By connecting various disciplines, we promote holistic approaches to understanding life in all its forms, exploring how biological processes interrelate and intersect with allied fields such as biotechnology, environmental sciences, and computational biology.

Scope

The Journal of Applied and Integrative Biology welcomes submissions from a wide range of life science disciplines, including but not limited to:

  • Molecular Biology
  • Genetics, Genomics and Proteomics
  • Ecology and Evolutionary Biology
  • Microbiology and Immunology
  • Biochemistry and Structural Biology
  • Systems Biology
  • Neurobiology and Behavior
  • Biotechnology and Bioengineering
  • Environmental and Conservation Biology
  • Computational Biology and Bioinformatics
  • Plant and Agricultural Biotechnology
  • Food Science and Nutrition
  • Nanotechnology and Bioelectronics
  • Medical Biotechnology

We are particularly interested in studies that bridge gaps between different areas, foster new collaborations, or present novel methodologies for solving complex biological problems.
